Free Fire Characters

Hayato
Samurai Spirit

A skilled samurai known for his ability, Bushido, which increases armor penetration as health decreases.

Kelly
Speedster

Kelly's Dash skill boosts her speed, allowing quick movement across the map and easy escapes.

Chrono
Time Turner

Chrono can create a force field that blocks damage, offering a unique defensive option in battles.

Alok
Healing Waves

DJ Alok’s Drop the Beat heals and boosts speed, essential for supporting teammates in tough situations.

Moco
Hacker's Eye

Moco tags enemies on hit, revealing their position temporarily, making her an ideal choice for strategic players.

Jota
Sustained Raids

Jota’s ability Sustained Raids grants health recovery with every gun kill, ideal for close-combat specialists.

Laura
Sharp Shooter

Laura is known for her Sharp Shooter skill, which enhances accuracy when scoped in.

K (Captain Booyah)
Master of All

K can switch between two modes: Jiu-jitsu (increases EP conversion) and Psychology (regenerates EP).

Skyler
Riptide Rhythm

Skyler’s Riptide Rhythm destroys Gloo Walls in range, making him effective against defensive plays.
