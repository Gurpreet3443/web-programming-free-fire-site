Free Fire Championship 2024

Tournament Overview

The Free Fire Championship 2024 brings together the top teams from around the world to compete for the ultimate prize and glory in the Free Fire esports scene!

Schedule

The championship runs from March 1 to March 30, 2024. Matches will be held weekly, culminating in the grand finals.

Rules

Teams consist of 4-5 players. Points are awarded based on kills and final placement in matches. All matches will be played in Battle Royale mode.
