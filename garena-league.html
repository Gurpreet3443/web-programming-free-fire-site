Garena Free Fire League 2024

Tournament Overview

The Garena Free Fire League 2024 is a premier tournament featuring top teams from various regions competing for a hefty prize pool and the title of champions.

Schedule

The league runs from February 1 to February 28, 2024. Matches will be streamed live on popular platforms.

Rules

Teams must consist of 5 players. Each match follows the Battle Royale format, with points awarded for kills and placement.
